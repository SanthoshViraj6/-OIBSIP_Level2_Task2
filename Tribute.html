Ram Pothineni

The Energetic Star

Ram Pothineni, the dynamic actor known for his high-energy performances in Telugu cinema.

Life and Career Timeline

  • 1988: Born on May 15th in Hyderabad, Telangana, India.
  • 2006: Debuts with the blockbuster movie "Devadasu".
  • 2008: Gains critical acclaim for "Ready".
  • 2011: Stars in "Kandireega", another hit film.
  • 2016: Impresses audiences with his performance in "Nenu Sailaja".
  • 2019: Delivers a standout performance in "iSmart Shankar".
  • 2020: Continues his success streak with "Red".
  • 2023: Announces new projects with leading directors and production houses.
  • 2024: Expands his repertoire with diverse and challenging roles.

Notable Achievements

Ram Pothineni is celebrated for his vibrant and energetic acting style, bringing life to his characters in movies like "Devadasu", "Ready", and "iSmart Shankar". His dedication to the craft and his dynamic screen presence have made him a beloved figure in Telugu cinema.
